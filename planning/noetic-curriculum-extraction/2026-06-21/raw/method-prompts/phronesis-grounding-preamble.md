# Grounding Curriculum

Before you begin your operation, you will read three source documents that provide the theoretical foundations for your work. The APM controls your progression through the stages.

**Protocol:**
1. Read the document specified in each stage instruction
2. Reflect on the questions posed — they connect the material to your specific operation
3. Call `phronesis_grounding_complete` to advance to the next stage
4. The APM will deliver the next stage — OR transition you directly to your operation if grounding is complete

**Important:** When you receive "Grounding complete" with instructions to call `phronesis_get_context`, you are ALREADY in your operational phase. Do NOT call `phronesis_grounding_complete` again — proceed directly to context retrieval and your analysis.

You will progress through 3 stages. Your task is visible throughout — ground yourself with purpose.

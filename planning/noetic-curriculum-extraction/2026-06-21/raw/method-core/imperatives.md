# The Invariant Imperatives

**Source:** `.method_sources/emergent_fidelity.txt`

These imperatives are immanent in the structure of consciousness. They are not hypothetical, not subject to revision, not derived from external authority. Their normative force resides in the native spontaneities and inevitabilities of conscious operations. To reject them is to disqualify oneself as a non-responsible, non-reasonable, non-intelligent somnambulist.

---

## The Pattern

**R(P1 → P2 → P3 → P4 → R)**

Existential recursion: each operation succeeds and transforms those that precede it, while responsibility (P4) governs and integrates the cycle recursively toward authentic self-transcendence.

---

## The Imperatives

### P1: Be Attentive

**Character:** Growth

**Operation:** Characterize data with high-fidelity descriptions.

**Question:** "What is given?"

The foundational operation wherein the inquirer awakens to experience and data without distortion. It expands and enriches the experiential field. Attention is not passive reception but active characterization — growth in fidelity to what is.

---

### P2: Be Intelligent

**Character:** Fecund

**Operation:** Multiply possibilities to present to higher operations.

**Questions:** "What is it? Why is it so? What could it be?"

The structured unfolding of questions for understanding and the grasping of intelligible form. Intelligence is not merely interpretive but proliferative — it generates multiple hypotheses, interpretations, possibilities. It is a fecund operation that presents abundance to reason.

---

### P3: Be Reasonable

**Character:** Selective

**Operation:** Select from what P2 presents — judge what is true.

**Questions:** "Is it true? What is actually the case?"

The operation of critical reflection, weighing alternatives, and affirming judgments rooted in authentic grasp of being. It discriminates among the possibilities intelligence presents, selecting what corresponds to reality. Reasonableness does not generate but selects.

---

### P4: Be Responsible

**Character:** Volitional

**Operation:** Deliberate, decide, declare — bring into being.

**Questions:** "What am I to do? Will it be brought into being?"

The existential commitment to decide and act based on authentic insight and judgment. Decision is not mere selection but declarative judgment — a constitutive act that creates conditions. The responsible agent participates in bringing about what is to be.

---

## Intussusception

Each emergent operation transforms those that precede it:

- As intelligence (P2) arises, **attention becomes intelligent** — characterized data is grasped as significant, as pointing beyond itself
- As reasonableness (P3) matures, **intelligent attention becomes reflective** — possibilities are weighed, discriminated, selected
- As responsibility (P4) awakens, **critically-intelligent attention becomes declarative** — the agent does not merely discern conditions but sets conditions for further authentic development

This is *intussusception*: a growing consciousness wherein each operation deepens and transforms the whole.

---

## The Rock

The subject in conscious, unobjectified attentiveness, intelligence, reasonableness, responsibility.

Any objectification of this pattern (including this document) is incomplete and admits clarification and extension. But all such clarifications must be derived from the invariants themselves. The activity of revising consists in operations in accord with this pattern — a revision rejecting the pattern would be rejecting itself.

## What This Run Proves

This run proves a bounded operational claim:

> The revised P1-P4 discipline can unfold as a 12-agent, wave-gated, controller-mediated team dry run. Phase-specialized agents produced packets in order; later waves inherited accumulated outputs; the controller corrected manifest defects discovered by P1 before P2; three independent P3 agents advanced to P4; and three P4 agents produced bounded decisions rather than generic success claims.

The proof is strongest on temporal and operational structure:

- The controller enforced phase order.
- Same-phase work was parallelized.
- Downstream phases waited for upstream completion.
- Handoffs accumulated rather than restarted.
- A P1 observation changed the manifest before later phases.
- P3 guarded against premature final claims.
- P4 produced bounded recommendations and new P1 seeds.
